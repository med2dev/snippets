fizzBuzz

fizzBuzz accepts a positive integer, num, and prints positive integers from 1 to num, replacing those divisible by 3 with 'fizz', those divisible by 5 with 'buzz', and those divisible by 15 by 'fizzBuzz'.

The output is printed to the console. The following test cases run on page open:

  1. fizzBuzz(3)
  2. fizzBuzz(5)
  3. fizzBuzz(15)
